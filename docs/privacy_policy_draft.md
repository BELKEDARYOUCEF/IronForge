# IronForge Privacy Policy Draft

Date: 13 juin 2026

IronForge is designed as an offline-first workout logger.

## Data Stored

The app may store the following data on your device:

- workout sessions;
- exercises and favorites;
- routines;
- onboarding preferences such as goal, level, units, frequency, and training type.

## Cloud Sync

IronForge does not currently use cloud sync or user accounts.

## Sensitive Permissions

IronForge does not currently request camera, photos, location, microphone, contacts, or health sensor permissions.

## Data Sharing

IronForge does not currently share workout data with third parties.

## Data Deletion

Current local data can be removed by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app. A built-in delete/export screen should be added before public release.

## Future Changes

If cloud sync, photos, body measurements, health integrations, subscriptions, or analytics are added, this policy must be updated before release.
